How to use Discount Voucher - a Step by Step Guide

This section explains about the Paisa Vasool Offer of BSNL-WOW service! It is EASY, follow to redeem and make the best out of it.

Step 1: Recharge

Recharge Online or via retailer with any of the following Recharge vouchers: Recharge 97, STV 198, STV 397 & STV 399. Offer valid across India except Jammu & Kashmir and Andhra Pradesh/Telangana Circle. 

Step 2: Receive Communication

Once successfully recharged, you will receive SMS communication with details of how to Access FREE BSNL-WOW Entertainment service and another SMS communication with details of offers along with your Discount Voucher. 

Step 3: Select the offer

You may receive multiple SMS with multi offers and multiple online retailers. You can select one or all offers at a time. 

Step 4: Visit Online Merchant

Click on the link provided in SMS and you will land on this page. You can select the retailer of your choice from this page and click on link to visit online retailer. 

Step 5: Select the Item you wish to Buy

Select the item (You can select more than One item at a time, there is no Limit) you wish to buy from the retailer.

Step 6: Checkout & Redeem offer

Proceed towards CHECK OUT Page. This is where retailer asks for your address and other details to ensure delivery. On CheckOut Page, apply the Coupon Code provided and click Apply. On successful apply, your total will now be reduced with value mentioned in the coupon code. Pay the discounted price and HURRAY you have successfully redeemed the offer.
